About Me!

I am Priyal Tripathi, a B.Sc Life Sciences student at Maitreyi College, University of Delhi. I am a bioinformatics and computational biology enthusiast with an interest in translational research in cancer genomics, as well as computational neuroscience. As I'm just a beginner, this website is not only my portfolio but also a blog! I plan on posting projects and the methodologies I used within them, as well as reviews of books and courses that I take up.

My love for animals runs deep, and one of my biggest life goals is to ensure they feel loved, safe, and happy. This passion even led me to establish a college society dedicated to animal welfare—Nepenthe—but that's a story for another time.
